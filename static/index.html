Who Are We?

Foodentity is developed by a small group of aspiring software developers, which consists of five computer science students from Laguna State Polytechnic University - San Pablo Campus.

What is Our Website About

Foodentity is an AI-powered appthat can analyze your meals instantly. Simply upload the image of your food, and we'll deliver precise nutritional insights to help you make smarter dietary choices effortlessly.

Why this Matters?

Many gym-goers struggle to achieve their fitness goals. Tracking nutrition often feels overwhelming—manually logging every meal and figuring out what your body needs can be confusing. This leads to inconsistent results, unbalanced diets, and wasted effort.

Our Journey

As a team of five trainees during our IBM immersion program, we developed FoodEntity, an AI-powered web app that identifies food from photos and provides nutritional information. Through challenges and collaboration, we grew as developers and learned the power of teamwork, turning our vision into reality.

What Makes Us Unique?

Foodentity leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to provide precise nutritional insights for every meal. Unlike traditional apps that rely on the manual input of food items, Foodentity uses advanced image classification to instantly identify your food and deliver tailored nutritional information with ease.
